Organic Bytes is a unique concept with a complete Sales/ Franchise model with low Investment and fast ROI.

If the sales partner reaches the target sales then the Franchise of pod is given to sales partners.


AREA :

The Sales partners can take franchise of particular location & can increase the pods in that particular location.


State:
